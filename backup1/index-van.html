12 Principles.

  • 1

    Open

    Open source, open API, open algorithm.

  • 2

    Work everywhere

    To the extent possible. Without consent.

  • 3

    Non-profit

    But financially sustainable.

  • 4

    Neutral

    Favor no ideological or political positions.

  • 5

    100% Community moderated

    Bottoms up, not top down.

  • 6

    Private

    Strong protections for privacy of all data.

  • 7

    Pseudonymous

    Credibility without public identity.

  • 8

    International

    From the ground up.

  • 9

    Transparent, auditable

    In systems. In governance.

  • 10

    Think long term

    Infrastructure for 100 years? 10,000?

  • 11

    Many formats, many contexts.

    HTML, PDF, video, books. News, blogs scientific articles, legislation, regulations, Terms of Service, ad inf.

  • 12

    Work with the best

    Remain humble.

Dan Whaley.

Dan is the coder and entrepreneur who built the first web-based travel reservation system and the company around it - Internet Travel Network (ITN)

The first airline reservation made over the web was booked via a server in his living room in 1995. The company (later renamed GetThere) went public in 1999 and was purchased in 2000 by Sabre. It still handles over 60% of the B2B market for online travel services and is one of the largest online transaction processing systems in the world.

Dan is Director of Sauce Labs and the founding advisor of GetAround, the 2011 TechCrunch Disrupt Cup winner.
