12 Principles:

1

Free

Free, open source software using open standards.

2

Work everywhere

To the extent practical. Without consent.

3

Non-profit

Sustained by social enterprise.

4

Neutral

Favor no ideological or political positions.

5

100% community moderated

Bottom up, not top down.

6

Merit based

Influenced based on track record.

7

Pseudonymous

Credibility without public identity.

8

International

By design.

9

Transparent, auditable

In systems. In governance.

10

Think long term

Infrastructure for 100 years. Or longer?

11

Many formats, many contexts

HTML, PDF, video, books. News, blogs, scientific articles, legislation, regulations, Terms of Service, etc.

12

Work with the best

Remain humble.
